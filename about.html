About Study O'Clock: Kerala's Leading Overseas Education Consultants

Our Mission: The Right Guide at the Right Time

Founded in Kochi, Study O'Clock has grown to become one of the most respected overseas education consultants in Kerala. Our mission is to empower students to make informed decisions at the most crucial time of their academic life.

Our philosophy is simple: combine deep industry expertise with a genuine passion for helping students achieve their dreams of studying abroad. We believe that every student deserves access to world-class education, and we're here to make that dream a reality.

With years of experience in guiding students towards top universities in the UK, Australia, Canada, and beyond, we have built a reputation for excellence and integrity in the overseas education consultancy sector.
