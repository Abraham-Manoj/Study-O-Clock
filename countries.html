Choose Your Study Destination

Explore top countries offering world-class education and post-study opportunities for international students from Kerala.

Study in United Kingdom

Home to world-renowned universities like Oxford and Cambridge, offering prestigious degrees recognized globally. Experience rich history and diverse culture.

  • Post-study work visa: 2 years
  • Top universities: Oxford, Cambridge, Imperial
  • Popular courses: Business, Engineering, Medicine

Study in Australia

Home to top-ranked universities, vibrant cities, and excellent post-study work rights. Learn more about becoming an international student in Australia.

  • Post-study work visa: 2-4 years
  • Top universities: ANU, Melbourne, Sydney
  • Popular courses: Engineering, IT, Healthcare

Study in Canada

A welcoming country with a high standard of living, excellent education system, and clear pathways to permanent residency.

  • Post-study work visa: Up to 3 years
  • Top universities: Toronto, UBC, McGill
  • Popular courses: Computer Science, Business

Study in Germany

World-class engineering education and tuition-free public universities. The heart of Europe with strong industrial connections.

  • Tuition: Free at public universities
  • Top universities: TUM, RWTH Aachen
  • Popular courses: Engineering, Research

Study in New Zealand

Known for its innovative education system, stunning landscapes, and friendly people. Excellent quality of life and research opportunities.

  • Post-study work visa: 1-3 years
  • Top universities: University of Auckland
  • Popular courses: Agriculture, Environment

Study in United States

Home to the world's top universities and cutting-edge research. Diverse academic programs and excellent career opportunities.

  • Optional Practical Training: 1-3 years
  • Top universities: Harvard, MIT, Stanford
  • Popular courses: Technology, Business, Medicine
